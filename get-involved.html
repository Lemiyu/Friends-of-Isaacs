Every pound goes directly to the shelves

Running five sheds, two vans, and a warehouse takes real funding. Every donation — however large or small — goes directly towards keeping the shelves stocked and the doors open, 365 days a year.

Donate Food

Drop it off at any shed, any day

All five sheds accept donations every day of the year. No need to call ahead — just drop off what you can at your nearest location.

We welcome any of the following items:

  • Tinned & dried goods
  • Jars & bottles
  • Breakfast items
  • Hot & cold drinks
  • Toiletries
  • Baby items
  • Cleaning products
  • Pet food
  • Period products

Volunteer

Join a team that's making a real difference

Friends of Isaac's runs on a team of 10–15 volunteers who give their time to keep collections running, sheds stocked, and deliveries moving. Without them, none of this would be possible.

If you can spare a few hours a week, we'd love to have you involved. No experience needed — just a willingness to help.

What you might do

  • Collect and sort donations
  • Stock and maintain sheds
  • Drive and deliver with the vans
  • Help at community events
  • Spread the word on social media

What we ask

  • A few hours a week
  • Reliability and commitment
  • A friendly, community spirit

Partnerships

Partner with us and help us scale

We're proud to work with some incredible organisations — from Aldi and Tesco to Jaguar Land Rover, Amazon, PepsiCo, and Persimmon Homes. Their support has helped us grow from one shed to five, and kept our shelves stocked through everything.

If your organisation wants to make a meaningful difference in Redditch, we'd love to talk — whether that's product donations, fundraising, employee volunteering, or a longer-term partnership.
