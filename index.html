Our Story

Started with pocket money, built on community

In November 2020, a nine-year-old called Isaac set up a pop-up greenhouse on his family's driveway in Redditch. No funding. No backing. Just his own pocket money and a belief that no one should go hungry. Five years on, Friends of Isaac's Food Bank is a registered CIC with two vans, a warehouse, and five sheds serving around 300 families every week.
